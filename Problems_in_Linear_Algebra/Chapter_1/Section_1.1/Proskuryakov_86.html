Định thức của hệ: \begin{align} \Delta & = \begin{vmatrix} 2a & -23 & 29 \\ 7 & a & 4 \\ 5 & 2 & a \end{vmatrix} \\ & = \left( {2a} \right)\left( a \right)\left( a \right) + \left( { - 23} \right)\left( 4 \right)\left( 5 \right) + \left( 7 \right)\left( 2 \right)\left( {29} \right) - \left( 5 \right)\left( a \right)\left( {29} \right) - \left( 7 \right)\left( { - 23} \right)\left( a \right) - \left( 2 \right)\left( 4 \right)\left( {2a} \right) \\ & = 2{a^3} - 460 + 406 - 145a + 161a - 16a = 2{a^3} - 54 = 2\left( {{a^3} - 27} \right) \end{align}

\(\begin{array}{ll} & \begin{vmatrix} a^2 & b\sin\alpha & c\sin\alpha \\ b\sin\alpha & 1 & \cos\alpha \\ c\sin\alpha & \cos\alpha & 1 \end{vmatrix} \\ = & \left( {{a^2}} \right)\left( 1 \right)\left( 1 \right) + \left( {b\sin \alpha } \right)\left( {\cos \alpha } \right)\left( {c\sin \alpha } \right) + \left( {b\sin \alpha } \right)\left( {\cos \alpha } \right)\left( {c\sin \alpha } \right) \\ & \hspace{1cm} - \left( {c\sin \alpha } \right)\left( 1 \right)\left( {c\sin \alpha } \right) - \left( {b\sin \alpha } \right)\left( {b\sin \alpha } \right)\left( 1 \right) - \left( {\cos \alpha } \right)\left( {\cos \alpha } \right)\left( {{a^2}} \right) \\ = & {a^2} + 2bc{\sin ^2}\alpha \cos \alpha - {c^2}{\sin ^2}\alpha - {b^2}{\sin ^2}\alpha - {a^2}{\cos ^2}\alpha \\ = & {a^2}\left( {1 - {{\cos }^2}\alpha } \right) + 2bc{\sin ^2}\alpha \cos \alpha - {b^2}{\sin ^2}\alpha - {c^2}{\sin ^2}\alpha \\ = & {a^2}{\sin ^2}\alpha + 2bc{\sin ^2}\alpha \cos \alpha - {b^2}{\sin ^2}\alpha - {c^2}{\sin ^2}\alpha \\ = & {\sin ^2}\alpha \times \left( {{a^2} - {b^2} - {c^2} + 2bc\cos \alpha } \right) \end{array}\)
Theo định lý hàm số \(\cos\) trong tam giác, ta có \(a^2 = b^2 + c^2 - 2bc\cos\alpha\). Từ đó dễ dàng suy ra định thức đã cho bằng~0.

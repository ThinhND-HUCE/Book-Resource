Định thức của hệ: \(\Delta = \begin{vmatrix} \tan\alpha & 1 \\ 1 & - \tan\alpha \end{vmatrix} = \tan\alpha\times \left( -\tan\alpha \right) - 1\times 1 = - \left( 1 + \tan^2\alpha \right) = - \dfrac{1}{\cos^2\alpha} \ne 0\Rightarrow \) hệ có nghiệm duy nhất.
\(\Delta _x = \begin{vmatrix} \sin\left( \alpha + \beta \right) & 1 \cos\left( \alpha + \beta \right) & - \tan\alpha \end{vmatrix} = \sin\left( \alpha + \beta \right)\times \left( - \tan\alpha \right) - \cos\left( \alpha + \beta \right)\times 1 = \sin\left( \alpha + \beta \right) \dfrac{-\sin\alpha}{\cos\alpha} - \cos\left( \alpha + \beta \right) = - \dfrac{\cos\left( \alpha + \beta \right) \cos\alpha + \sin\left( \alpha + \beta \right) \sin\alpha}{\cos\alpha} = \) \( - \dfrac{\cos\left[\left( \alpha + \beta \right) - \alpha \right]}{\cos\alpha} = - \dfrac{\cos\beta}{\cos\alpha} \Rightarrow x = \dfrac{\Delta _x}{\Delta} = \dfrac{- \dfrac{\cos\beta}{\cos\alpha}}{- \dfrac{1}{\cos^2\alpha}} = \cos\alpha \cos\beta \)
\(\Delta _y = \begin{vmatrix} \tan\alpha & \sin\left( \alpha + \beta \right) 1 & \cos\left( \alpha + \beta \right) \end{vmatrix} = \tan\alpha\times \cos\left( \alpha + \beta \right) - 1\times \sin\left( \alpha + \beta \right) = \) \(\dfrac{\sin\alpha}{\cos\alpha} \cos\left( \alpha + \beta \right) - \sin\left( \alpha + \beta \right) = - \dfrac{\sin\left( \alpha + \beta \right) \cos\alpha - \cos\left( \alpha + \beta \right) \sin\alpha}{\cos\alpha} = \) \(- \dfrac{\sin\left[\left( \alpha + \beta \right) - \alpha \right]}{\cos\alpha} = - \dfrac{\sin\beta}{\cos\alpha} \Rightarrow y = \dfrac{\Delta _y}{\Delta} = \dfrac{- \dfrac{\sin\beta}{\cos\alpha}}{- \dfrac{1}{\cos^2\alpha}} = \cos\alpha \sin\beta \)

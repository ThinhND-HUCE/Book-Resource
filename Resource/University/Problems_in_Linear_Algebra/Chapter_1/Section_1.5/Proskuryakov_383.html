Ký hiệu định thức đã cho là \(D\). \binom{p+2n+2}{n} & \binom{p+2n+3}{n} & \ldots & \binom{p+3n-1}{n} & \binom{p+3n}{n} \end{vmatrix} \] Từ cột thứ hai, trừ mỗi cột cho cột trước, sử dụng công thức Pascal \[ \binom{n}{k} = \binom{p+2n}{n-1} \\ \binom{p+n+2}{n} & \binom{p+n+2}{n-1} & \binom{p+n+3}{n-1} & \binom{p+n+4}{n-1} & \ldots & \binom{p+2n}{n-1} & \binom{p+2n+1}{n-1} \\ \binom{p+n+3}{n} & \binom{p+n+3}{n-1} & \binom{p+n+4}{n-1} & \binom{p+n+5}{n-1} & \ldots & \binom{p+2n+1}{n-1} & \binom{p+2n+2}{n-1} \\ \ldots & \ldots & \ldots & \ldots & \ldots & \ldots & \ldots \\ \binom{p+2n-1}{n} & \binom{p+2n-1}{n-1} & \binom{p+2n}{n-1} & \binom{p+2n+1}{n-1} & \ldots & \binom{p+3n-3}{n-1} & \binom{p+3n-2}{n-1} \\ \binom{p+2n}{n} & \binom{p+2n}{n-1} & \binom{p+2n+1}{n-1} & \binom{p+2n+2}{n-1} & \ldots & \binom{p+3n-2}{n-1} & \binom{p+3n-1}{n-1} \end{vmatrix} \] Trong định thức trên, từ cột thứ ba, trừ mỗi cột cho cột trước \[ D = \begin{vmatrix} \binom{p+n}{n} & \binom{p+n}{n-1} & \binom{p+n}{n-2} & \binom{p+n+1}{n-2} & \ldots & \binom{p+2n-3}{n-2} & \binom{p+2n-2}{n-2} \\ \binom{p+n+1}{n} & \binom{p+n+1}{n-1} & \binom{p+n+1}{n-2} & \binom{p+n+2}{n-2} & \ldots & \binom{p+2n-2}{n-2} & \binom{p+2n-1}{n-2} \\ \binom{p+n+2}{n} & \binom{p+n+2}{n-1} & \binom{p+n+2}{n-2} & \binom{p+n+3}{n-2} & \ldots & \binom{p+2n-1}{n-2} & \binom{p+2n}{n-2} \\ \binom{p+n+3}{n} & \binom{p+n+3}{n-1} & \binom{p+3n-3}{n-2} & \binom{p+3n-2}{n-2} \end{vmatrix} \] Trong định thức thu được, từ cột thứ tư, trừ mỗi cột cho cột trước \[ D = \begin{vmatrix} \binom{p+n}{n} & \binom{p+n}{n-1} & \binom{p+n}{n-2} & \binom{p+n}{n-3} & \ldots & \binom{p+2n-4}{n-3} & \binom{p+2n-3}{n-3} \\ \binom{p+n+1}{n} & \binom{p+n+1}{n-1} & \binom{p+n+1}{n-2} & \binom{p+n+1}{n-3} & \ldots & \ldots & \binom{p+3n-4}{n-3} & \binom{p+3n-3}{n-3} \end{vmatrix} \] Cứ tiếp tục như vậy, ta được \[ D = \begin{vmatrix} \binom{p+n}{n} & \binom{p+n}{n-1} & \binom{p+n}{n-2} & \binom{p+n}{n-3} & \ldots & \binom{p+n}{1} & \binom{p+n}{0} \\ \binom{p+n+1}{n} & \binom{p+n+1}{n-1} & \binom{p+n+1}{n-2} & \binom{p+n+1}{n-3} & \ldots & \binom{p+n+1}{1} & \binom{p+n+1}{0} \\ \binom{p+n+2}{n} & \binom{p+n+2}{n-1} & \binom{p+n+2}{n-2} & \binom{p+n+2}{n-3} & \ldots & \binom{p+n+2}{1} & \binom{p+n+2}{0} \\ \binom{p+n+3}{n} & \binom{p+n+3}{n-1} & \binom{p+n+3}{n-2} & \binom{p+n+3}{n-3} & \ldots & \binom{p+n+3}{1} & \binom{p+n+3}{0} \\ \ldots & \ldots & \ldots & \ldots & \ldots & \ldots & \ldots \\ \binom{p+2n-1}{n} & \binom{p+2n-1}{n-1} & \binom{p+2n-1}{n-2} & \binom{p+2n-1}{n-3} & \ldots & \binom{p+2n-1}{1} & \binom{p+2n-1}{0} \\ \binom{p+2n}{n} & \binom{p+2n}{n-1} & \binom{p+2n}{n-2} & \binom{p+2n}{n-3} & \ldots & \binom{p+2n}{1} & \binom{p+2n}{0} \end{vmatrix} \] Thực hiện phép biến đổi tương tự như trên, nhưng áp dụng đối với hàng. Từ hàng thứ hai trở đi, trừ mỗi hàng cho hàng trên. Trong định thức mới, từ hàng thứ ba, lại trừ mỗi hàng cho hàng trên,\(\ldots\), và lưu ý \(\displaystyle \binom{n}{0} = 1\), ta có \begin{align} D & = \begin{vmatrix} \binom{p+n}{n} & \binom{p+n}{n-1} & \binom{p+n}{n-2} & \binom{p+n}{n-3} & \ldots & \binom{p+n}{1} & 1 \\ \binom{p+n}{n-1} & \binom{p+n}{n-2} & \binom{p+n}{n-3} & \binom{p+n}{n-4} & \ldots & \binom{p+n}{0} & 0 \\ \binom{p+n+1}{n-1} & \binom{p+2n-2}{0} & 0 \\ \binom{p+2n-1}{n-1} & \binom{p+2n-1}{n-2} & \binom{p+2n-1}{n-3} & \binom{p+2n-1}{n-4} & \ldots & \binom{p+2n-1}{0} & 0 \end{vmatrix} \\ & = \begin{vmatrix} \binom{p+n}{n} & \binom{p+n}{n-1} & \binom{p+n}{n-2} & \binom{p+n}{n-3} & \ldots & \binom{p+n}{1} & 1 \\ \binom{p+n}{n-1} & \binom{p+n}{n-2} & \binom{p+n}{n-3} & \binom{p+n}{n-4} & \ldots & 1 & 0 \\ \binom{p+n}{n-2} & \binom{p+n}{n-3} & \binom{p+n}{n-4} & \binom{p+n}{n-5} & \ldots & 1 & 0 \\ \binom{p+n+1}{n-2} & \binom{p+n+1}{n-3} & \binom{p+n+1}{n-4} & \binom{p+n+1}{n-5} & \ldots & 0 & 0 \\ \ldots & \ldots & \ldots & \ldots & \ldots & \ldots & \ldots \\ \binom{p+2n-3}{n-2} & \binom{p+2n-3}{n-3} & \binom{p+2n-3}{n-4} & \binom{p+2n-3}{n-5} & \ldots & 0 & 0 \\ \binom{p+2n-2}{n-2} & \binom{p+2n-2}{n-3} & \binom{p+2n-2}{n-4} & \binom{p+2n-2}{n-5} & \ldots & 0 & 0 \end{vmatrix} \\ & = \begin{vmatrix} \binom{p+n}{n} & \binom{p+n}{n-1} & \binom{p+n}{n-2} & \binom{p+n}{n-3} & \ldots & \binom{p+n}{1} & 1 \\ \binom{p+n}{n-1} & \binom{p+n}{n-2} & \binom{p+n}{n-3} & \binom{p+n}{n-4} & \ldots & 1 & 0 \\ \binom{p+n}{n-2} & \binom{p+n}{n-3} & \binom{p+n}{n-4} & \binom{p+n}{n-5} & \ldots & 0 & 0 \\ \binom{p+n}{n-3} & \binom{p+n}{n-4} & \binom{p+n}{n-5} & \binom{p+n}{n-6} & \ldots & 0 & 0 \\ \ldots & \ldots & \ldots & \ldots & \ldots & \ldots & \ldots \\ \binom{p+2n-4}{n-3} & \binom{p+2n-4}{n-4} & \binom{p+2n-4}{n-5} & \binom{p+2n-4}{n-6} & \ldots & 0 & 0 \\ \binom{p+2n-3}{n-3} & \binom{p+2n-3}{n-4} & \binom{p+2n-3}{n-5} & \binom{p+2n-3}{n-6} & \ldots & 0 & 0 \end{vmatrix} = \ldots \\ & = \begin{vmatrix} \binom{p+n}{n} & \binom{p+n}{n-1} & \binom{p+n}{n-2} & \binom{p+n}{n-3} & \ldots & \binom{p+n}{1} & 1 \\ \binom{p+n}{n-1} & \binom{p+n}{n-2} & \binom{p+n}{n-3} & \binom{p+n}{n-4} &

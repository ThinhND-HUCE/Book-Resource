Bài 384:

Ký hiệu định thức đã cho là \(D_n\). Ta viết lại \(D_n\) chi tiết hơn. \[ D_n = \begin{vmatrix} 1 & \binom{p}{1} & \binom{p}{2} & \binom{p}{3} & \ldots & \binom{p}{n-1} & \binom{p}{n} \\ 1 & \binom{p+1}{1} & \binom{p+1}{2} & \binom{p+1}{3} & \ldots & \binom{p+1}{n-1} & \binom{p+1}{n} \\ 1 & \binom{p+2}{1} & \binom{p+2}{2} & \binom{p+2}{3} & \ldots & \binom{p+2}{n-1} & \binom{p+2}{n} \\ 1 & \binom{p+3}{1} & \binom{p+3}{2} & \binom{p+3}{3} & \ldots & \binom{p+3}{n-1} & \binom{p+3}{n} \\ \ldots & \ldots & \ldots & \ldots & \ldots & \ldots & \ldots \\ 1 & \binom{p+n-1}{1} & \binom{p+n-1}{2} & \binom{p+n-1}{3} & \ldots & \binom{p+n-1}{n-1} & \binom{p+n-1}{n} \\ 1 & \binom{p+n}{1} & \binom{p+n}{2} & \binom{p+n}{3} & \ldots & \binom{p+n}{n-1} & \binom{p+n}{n} \end{vmatrix} \] Từ cột thứ hai, trừ mỗi cột cho cột trước, sử dụng công thức Pascal \[ \binom{n}{k} = \binom{n-1}{k} + \binom{n-1}{k-1} \quad\Rightarrow\quad \binom{n}{k} - \binom{n-1}{k} = \binom{n-1}{k-1}, \] ta có \[ D_n = \begin{vmatrix} 1 & \binom{p}{1} & \binom{p}{2} & \binom{p}{3} & \ldots & \binom{p}{n-1} & \binom{p}{n} \\ 0 & \binom{p}{0} & \binom{p}{1} & \binom{p}{2} & \ldots & \binom{p}{n-2} & \binom{p}{n-1} \\ 0 & \binom{p+1}{0} & \binom{p+1}{1} & \binom{p+1}{2} & \ldots & \binom{p+1}{n-2} & \binom{p+1}{n-1} \\ 0 & \binom{p+2}{0} & \binom{p+2}{1} & \binom{p+2}{2} & \ldots & \binom{p+2}{n-2} & \binom{p+2}{n-1} \\ \ldots & \ldots & \ldots & \ldots & \ldots & \ldots & \ldots \\ 0 & \binom{p+n-2}{0} & \binom{p+n-2}{1} & \binom{p+n-2}{2} & \ldots & \binom{p+n-2}{n-2} & \binom{p+n-2}{n-1} \\ 0 & \binom{p+n-1}{0} & \binom{p+n-1}{1} & \binom{p+n-1}{2} & \ldots & \binom{p+n-1}{n-2} & \binom{p+n-1}{n-1} \end{vmatrix} \] Khai triển định thức theo cột đầu, lưu ý \(\displaystyle \binom{n}{0} = 1\), ta lại thu được \(\left( 1 \right) D_{n-1}\), nên \[ D_n = D_{n-1} \] với \(n\ge 2\). Do đó \[ D_n = D_1 = \begin{vmatrix} 1 & \binom{p}{1} \\ 1 & \binom{p+1}{1} \end{vmatrix} = \left( 1 \right) \left( p+1 \right) - \left( 1 \right) \left( p \right) = 1 . \]

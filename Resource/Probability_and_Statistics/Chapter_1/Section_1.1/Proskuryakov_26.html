Định thức của hệ: \(\Delta = \begin{vmatrix} \cos\alpha & -\sin\alpha \\ \sin\alpha & \cos\alpha \end{vmatrix} = \cos\alpha \times \cos\alpha - \sin\alpha\times\left( -\sin\alpha \right) = \cos^2\alpha + \sin^2\alpha = 1\ne 0\Rightarrow \) hệ có nghiệm duy nhất.
\(\Delta _x = \begin{vmatrix} \cos\beta & -\sin\alpha \\ \sin\beta & \cos\alpha \end{vmatrix} = \cos\beta \times \cos\alpha - \sin\beta\times\left( -\sin\alpha \right) = \cos\alpha \cos\beta + \sin\alpha \sin\beta = \cos\left( \alpha - \beta \right) \Rightarrow x = \dfrac{\Delta _x}{\Delta} = \dfrac{\cos\left( \alpha - \beta \right)}{1} = \cos\left( \alpha - \beta \right) \)
\(\Delta _y = \begin{vmatrix} \cos\alpha & \cos\beta \\ \sin\alpha & \sin\beta \end{vmatrix} = \cos\alpha\times \sin\beta - \sin\alpha \times \cos\beta = \sin\beta \cos\alpha - \cos\beta \sin\alpha = \sin\left( \beta - \alpha \right) \Rightarrow y = \dfrac{\Delta _y}{\Delta} = \dfrac{\sin\left( \beta - \alpha \right)}{1} = \sin\left( \beta - \alpha \right) \)
